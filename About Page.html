Founder & CEO
Meet the founder

Ronaldo Padua

U.S. Army Veteran · Federal HR Specialist · Founder of PaduaCo & One Percent Consulting

Ronaldo Padua is a U.S. Army veteran and federal Human Resources Specialist who has dedicated his career to giving federal employees the clarity, strategy, and confidence they need to take full control of their benefits.

He founded PaduaCo in 2011 as the engine behind everything he builds — digital systems, educational tools, and high-impact training programs. In 2015, he launched One Percent Consulting, the specialized branch of PaduaCo created to deliver elite, no-nonsense benefits guidance to federal employees nationwide.

Ronaldo has led workshops, conferences, and 1-on-1 sessions across the United States and Puerto Rico, empowering thousands of federal employees to eliminate confusion, avoid costly mistakes, and make decisions from a position of strength.

As both a veteran and a federal HR professional, he understands the system from the inside — and he knows how easily employees can be disadvantaged by unclear information. His mission is bold and simple: no federal employee should ever be left without the knowledge or support they deserve.
