How to read these. Each option uses a different typographic strategy — Roman inscriptional caps (01), italic initial (02), classical seal (03), high-contrast Italian serif (04), interlocked monogram (05), or display Italianate caps on black (06). All are restrained on purpose. Color is intentionally limited to ink, ivory, and a single brass accent — none of them shout.
