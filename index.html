IThe Problem

The three mistakes that cost federal employees the most.

Most aren't dramatic. They're small, technical, easy to miss — and once they're locked in, they don't unwind.

i.

You don't know what FEHB will actually cost you in retirement.

Your health coverage doesn't end when you retire — but the premiums change. Most people find out their real number after they've already signed the paperwork.

Typical impact: $2,000–$6,000 a year
ii.

You carry FEGLI past retirement without knowing the curve.

FEGLI premiums climb steeply after retirement. Many retirees stay enrolled out of habit and quietly pay several times what the same coverage costs elsewhere.

Typical impact: $1,000–$5,000 a year
iii.

You pick the wrong TSP withdrawal strategy — once.

Withdraw, leave it, roll it. Each path has different tax, access and survivor consequences. Once you choose, you usually can't undo it.

Typical impact: five figures, often more
